Simple Deviled Eggs

Ingredients for simple deviled eggs

  • 15 jumbo eggs
  • 3/4 cup cooked and chopped bacon, divided
  • 1/3 cup minced onion
  • 3 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoons bacon drippings
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Directions for the recipe

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water. bring to a boil; remove from heat and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes. Remove eggs from hot water. Cool under running water; peel eggs. Slice each egg in lengthwise. Place yolks in a small bowl.
  2. Mix 1/2 cup bacon, onion, dill picle relish, mayonnaise, mustard, and bacon grease with eggs yolks using a fork; keep the yolks chunky. Sitr in salt and black pepper.
  3. Scoop yolk mixture into 24 egg white halves. Sprinkle paprika and remaining 1/4 cup bacon on top.
