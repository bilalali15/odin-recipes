Soft Pretzel Bites

Ingredients for soft pretzel bites

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 3 quarts cold water
  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • Cooking spray
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water

Directions for the recipe

  1. Place 1 1/2 cups water, butter, brown sugar, yeast, and salt in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with hook attachment. Mix until well combined. Let sit for about 5 minutes.
  2. Turn mixer on low speed and add 4 cups flour, 1 cup at a time. Add additional 1/2 cup flour if dough appears sticky. Increase speed to medium and knead until dough is smooth and pulls away from the sides of the bowl, 4 to 5 minutes. Sprinkle in more flour if dough still seems a little tacky.
  3. Oil a large mixing bowl. Add the ball od dough. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise in warm place for about 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Grease a sheets pan with cooking spray
  5. Bring 3 quarts water to a boil in a large saucepan. Slowly add baking soda and return to a boil.
  6. Meanwhile, remove dough from the bowl, flatten into a disc shape, and cut into 8 wedges. Roll each wedge into a 24-inch rope. Cut each rope into 1-inch pieces.
  7. Brop batches of dough pieces into boiling water for about 35 seconds. Remove to the prepared sheet pan. Beat egg with 1 tablespoon water; brush over the tops each boiled dough piece. Repeat with remaining dough.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 14 to 16 minutes. Cool slightly before serving.
